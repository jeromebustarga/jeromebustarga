CSDJ Brand Identity

At the intersection of algorithms, aesthetics, and storytelling, CSDJ bridges technology, design, and journalism. This comprehensive brand system represents our commitment to innovation, creativity, and ethical communication.

Logo System

The primary CSDJ logo features a global network design that symbolizes worldwide digital connectivity. It consists of a white globe with latitude and longitude lines contained within a rounded square frame. The "CSDJ" letters wrap around the globe, creating a dynamic sense of movement. Connection points extend outward from the circuit-board inspired frame, representing data flow and digital networks, with color accents (cyan, magenta, and yellow) referencing the creative and technical aspects of our disciplines.
